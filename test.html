About Me

My name is Dana Hlavacova, a passionate Junior Software Developer and Tutor. I embarked on my journey in the tech industry after obtaining a bachelor's degree in Finance from the Mendel University in Brno. I've been honing my skills in JavaScript, PHP, ReactJS, and many more for the past year. I find it exciting that even a small piece of code can have a significant impact and help many people.

My enthusiasm for programming extends beyond writing code. I enjoy guiding others on their coding journey as a tutor at the Digital Career Institute GmbH. This role has not only deepened my understanding of web development but also allowed me to refine my communication and leadership skills. It's gratifying to see my students grow and even become tutors themselves.

Aside from coding, I'm a Czech native speaker who's also fluent in Slovak and proficient in German. My English is at an elementary level, and I'm constantly working on improving it. In my spare time, I find solace in gardening, cycling, and reading books, which bring relaxation and further personal growth.
